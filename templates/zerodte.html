Anomaly Type Reference

PARITY Parity Violation
ITM option's bid price is below its intrinsic value by >$0.10. Example: Stock at $150, Call strike $145 — intrinsic = $5.00, but bid = $4.80. Can indicate a genuine arbitrage or a quoting error.
STALE Stale Pricing
Last trade price is >30% away from current bid/ask midpoint. Option traded at a different price before the market moved. Watch for fills at stale prices.
HIGH IV Abnormal IV
Implied volatility exceeds 500% annualized with real volume. On expiration day this can indicate extreme positioning or a pricing model artifact on near-zero-time options.
WIDE SPREAD Wide Bid-Ask
Bid-ask spread exceeds 60% of midpoint on a liquid underlying with volume. Market maker may be widening quotes due to uncertainty.
UNUSUAL VOLUME Unusual Volume
High volume on a nearly-worthless strike (delta < 0.05). May indicate informed buying of a "lottery ticket" or unusual positioning.
